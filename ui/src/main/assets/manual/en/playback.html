Playback

You can listen back to your score by pressing the playback button in the top right - to stop playback just press it again.

You can pause the playback by long-pressing the playback button - when you press it again it will resume from the same position.

To start playback from a particular point in the score, long-press on the required segment, then press play.

To listen to a selection, long-press to set the start of the selection, single-press to set the end, then press play.

The mixer button brings up a mixer console, where the volumes of the various instruments can be altered, and muted or soloed:

There is also a panel of options to affect the playback

The leftmost button causes the score to be played in shuffle rhythm - ie simple eighth notes are played with a triplet feel.

The button allows playback of harmonies. When this button is long-pressed, an instrument selector appears to its right allowing you to select the sound of the playback. (Note this button won't have any effect if there are no chord symbols to play!)

The button allows looping of playback - if a range has been selected it will loop this section, otherwise it will keep playing the whole piece.
