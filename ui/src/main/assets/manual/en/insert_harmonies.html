Tap on the score to position the marker, or use the left and right buttons to move it.

Use the selectors to create the desired harmony symbol, then use the button to enter it - the marker will move to the next position.

A selection of commonly required harmonies in the current key signature appear in the 'Common' box - just tap on one to enter it at the marker.

Your most recently entered harmonies appear in the 'Recent' box - just tap on one to enter it at the marker.

If you wish to enter more than one harmony over a single long note, you can split the bar up with the button, which will create a dotted barline mid-way through the bar where the marker can be positioned. Long press on this button to remove any dotted barlines.

Use the eraser key to delete harmonies at the marker.
