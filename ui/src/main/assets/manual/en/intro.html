Basic Navigation

The first thing you see on starting the app is an empty score with one part, and a keyboard to enter the notes - just press on the keys and watch them come out on the score.

Learn more about note input here

You can zoom in and out of the score by the standard pinch-zoom gesture (as you would with Google maps).

Double tap anywhere in the score to restore the view to its standard width.

Long-pressing on a segment of the score makes the start of a selection, and brings up the clipboard panel - find out more here

To minimise the keyboard and get a better view of your creation, press the 'minimize' button.

To restore the keybord, just press this button again.
